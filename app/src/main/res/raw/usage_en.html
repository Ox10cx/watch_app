App Introduction

Thank you for choosing the tag alarm device from us. This device can be connected to mobile phones and tablets which support Bluetooth BLE 4.0 standard. You could pair it on wallet, handbag, key, child, old man or pet, etc., next you can find them by operating on your mobile phone or tablet.

Device Operation

  • Device turn-on: Press and hold the button on device for 3-5 seconds until you hear the sound 'Di-Di'.
  • Device turn-off: Press and hold the button on device for 3-5 seconds until you hear the sound 'Di'.

Connecting device

  • Click ‘Searching Devices’ icon, then you’ll see the devices waiting for connection.
  • As showed in the picture ‘Not Connected’, please click ‘Connect’ icon.

Warning Settings

  • Double click the button on device to activate the alarm function of the App.
  • When the alarm is on, click the red zone of alarming to stop it.
  • Click 'Alarm' icon to start or stop the alarm.
  • You can set the alarm when the ‘Anti-Lost’ function is turned on.

Camera

Click camera icon to enter camera mode. The 'Settings' icon is located at bottom-left corner. Click the button on device to take a photo.

Location

Click the Location Button to go into the map mode and get the current location. The location history and lost history are also available.

Settings

Enter the 'Settings' menu to configure the device. 'Do Not Disturb' mode: As showed in the picture, when the 'Do Not Disturb' mode is on, the alarm function would be deactivated.
  • 'Password protection': When the 'Password protection' function is enable, you need to input the correct password before entering the App; When the 'Password protection' function is disable, you can enter the App directly.
  • Map Select: You can select from 'Baidu Map' and 'Google Map' as you wish
  • Version: To check the current App version.
